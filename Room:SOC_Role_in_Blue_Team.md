## Meet the blue team
#### Blue team is focused on defensive security. Blue team constantly monitors for attacks and tries to respond to them ASAP
#### CIRT, Cyber Incident response Team (OR CERT OR CSIRT). Think of them as the firefighters for cyber. If a SOC expertise isn't enough, or incident is out of control, then it's best to contact a CIRT.
## Specialized Defensive Roles
#### * DevSecOps * Penetration Tester * GRC Auditor * AppSec Engineer * Threat Analyst * AI Researcher
#### Most companies now require specialized blue team roles. Ones that require a deeper understanding and broad experiences in broader fields. Special roles like * Digital Forensics Analyst * Threat Intelligence Analyst * AppSec Engineer *AI Researcher
